Ahmad R. Pratama

About

I am an Assistant Professor at Universitas Islam Indonesia, Department of Informatics where I am serving as the Secretary of the Department, currently living in Yogyakarta, Indonesia. Previously, I used to live in Long Island, NY, USA and Clayton, VIC, Australia.


I did my Ph.D. in Technology, Policy, and Innovation at Stony Brook University with Fulbright scholarship while also taking some courses at New York University and Teachers College, Columbia University under Inter-University Doctoral Consortium. I earned my Master's degree in Information Technology from Monash University and my Bachelor of Engineering degree from Universitas Gadjah Mada.

My research interests are mainly related to mobile technology and society, ranging from mobile apps, mobile enterprise solution, m-commerce, m-learning, mobile data analytics, mobile device security & forensics.

Apart from being academia, I love swimming, watching movies and AC Milan matches, as well as playing games on PC and consoles in my spare time.

Projects

Below are some of the projects I am currently working on, either with my co-authors from Indonesia and other countries or as a supervisor of my students.

Social Media and Identity

Using big data, machine learning, and deep learning, my team and I are trying to explore social identity in real life from the use of social media.

Security Awareness and Digital Literacy

Using survey data, my students and I are trying to investigate the level of security awareness and digital literacy of Indonesian people on the Internet, ranging from the existence hate speech, the danger of phishing, and the need for security update on smartphones and mobile apps.

Gig Economy, Financial Technology, and Digital Wallet

Using Exploratory Data Analysis and Structural Equation Modelling, my students and I are trying to explore participation in gig economy, and investigate factors affecting the acceptance of digital wallet and other financial technology in Indonesia

Evaluating Educational Policy amid COVID-19 Pandemic

Using big data from Facebook and Instagram with the help of CrowdTangle, my students and I are conducting sentiment analysis on the educational policy in Indonesia, such as learning from home amid COVID-19 pandemic
